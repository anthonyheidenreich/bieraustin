=== Surface – Free WordPress Theme ===

Surface is a simple and clean WordPress Theme. Easy Customize through Theme Options. Surface features: custom background, drop-down menu, header logo, highly customizable and adaptable, theme options, post excerpts with thumbnails, SEO friendly, translation ready, W3C valid, widget-ready, threaded comments & more. It is tested major browsers – Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer, Opera, Safari and Chrome. Surface is suitable for any business or personal website.
